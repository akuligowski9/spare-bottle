# Safety & Disclaimer

## Important Notice
Spare Bottle provides general information about alcoholic beverages, ingredients, and drink recipes.

This application is for informational and educational purposes only.

---

## No Advice
Spare Bottle does NOT provide:
- Medical advice
- Health guidance
- Legal advice
- Safety recommendations

No content in this app should be interpreted as guidance on safe alcohol consumption.

---

## Individual Responsibility
Alcohol affects individuals differently based on many factors not accounted for by this application.

Users are solely responsible for their decisions regarding alcohol use.

---

## Explicit Disclaimers
- This app does not assess tolerance, impairment, or safety
- This app does not recommend consumption levels
- This app does not track or evaluate behavior
- This app does not determine legal or driving limits

---

## Critical Reminder
**Never drink and drive.**

If you feel unwell or unsafe, stop drinking and seek appropriate assistance.

---

## Design Intent
Spare Bottle is intentionally designed to be calm, neutral, and non-promotional.
It exists to support awareness through information, not encouragement or persuasion.
